    Florian gave the instructions deduced by its study of the race scenarios to Sofiène who implemented them. Thibault implemented the final code, dealing with the bluetooth protocol, and added the elements from Sofiène at the appropriate places (i.e after START message, NEXT messages, or before BALL message).

    Moreover, Thibault was working using the bluetooth while Sofiene was working using the USB cable.
